The compiler

A mathematical campaign in. A current residual out.

Cutset walks backward from a named goal through registered theorem-grade routes. It preserves hypotheses, excludes only formulations carrying admitted negative evidence, and emits the unresolved leaves with provenance.

INPUTRegistered campaign

Goal, proved routes, exact assumptions, open obligations, and scoped walls.

SCOPE
Cutset computes from registered state. It does not prove that the registry is complete, that a proposed wall is true, or that every mathematical route has been represented.
The two non-upgrade laws

The graph cannot award itself credit.

Cutset may organize evidence and propose work. Status moves require separate admission.

OPEN ≠ THEOREM

Only the checker promotes proof status.

An agent, panel, graph path, or confident paragraph cannot make a theorem. The named deterministic gate decides.

ATTEMPT ≠ WALL

Failure to find is not evidence of exclusion.
